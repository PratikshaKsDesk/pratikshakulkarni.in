Pavanraje BMS – SaaS for Multistate Business Operations

Context

Managing daily attendance, late marks, expenses, and customer loan applications was highly manual for multistate businesses, consuming significant time, effort, and cost. Additionally, many employees had low technical literacy, making existing CMS portals complex and underused. The goal was to create a simplified, mobile-first solution for smoother business operations and better user adoption.

Solution

We built Pavanraje BMS as a user-friendly SaaS product, designed around real workflows and mobile-first adoption. The pilot app simplified attendance, expense management, and loan tracking, making operations faster and easier for staff with limited technical experience.

About Product

Developed for Pavanraje Multistate and Aalekkh Studio, this product is a purpose-built SaaS platform tailored for cooperative institutions. It helped businesses transition from manual processes to automated, mobile-based workflows while remaining accessible to low-tech users.
