Description

The Apex Pro Full Face helmet represents the pinnacle of MotoTrek's commitment to rider safety without compromising on style or comfort. Engineered for the serious adventurer, this helmet combines race-proven protection technology with the aerodynamic efficiency needed for long-distance touring.

The multi-density EPS liner system provides superior impact absorption across all impact zones, while the fiberglass composite shell delivers an optimal strength-to-weight ratio. The advanced ventilation system with 5 intake and 2 exhaust ports ensures maximum airflow, keeping you cool and focused on challenging rides.

Where Every Journey Begins

MotoTrek was born from a simple belief: the best adventures happen when you're equipped with gear you can trust and surrounded by people who share your passion for exploration.

We started as a small group of riders and trekkers frustrated with gear that couldn't keep up with our ambitions. Today, we've grown into a community of over 2,500 adventurers across India, but our mission remains unchanged—to provide premium equipment and unforgettable experiences for those who refuse to stay on the beaten path.

Since 2019

Our Mission

To equip adventurers with gear that performs as hard as they do, while building a community that celebrates the spirit of exploration over destination.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted adventure gear brand—not the biggest, but the one riders and trekkers recommend to their friends without hesitation.

Community First

We believe the best gear is worthless without stories to tell. That's why we invest as much in group rides and treks as we do in product development.
