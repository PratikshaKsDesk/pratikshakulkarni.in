üìñ Portfolio Guidelines

1
Include 3-5 Projects: Choose your best work that demonstrates different PM skills (strategy, execution, analytics, research).
2
Case Study Format: For each project, clearly explain the problem, your approach, solution, and measurable impact.
3
Show Real Work: Include actual artifacts (PRDs, wireframes, data analysis, research findings) - not just descriptions.
4
Quantify Results: Use specific metrics (e.g., "increased engagement by 35%" not just "improved engagement").
5
Professional Presentation: Use clear formatting, proper grammar, and visual aids. Make it easy to scan and understand.
6
Respect Confidentiality: Remove sensitive company data, user information, or proprietary details. Use anonymized versions.
