Online Parayan – Digital Spiritual Community

Context

Thousands of devotees deeply value spiritual texts like the Dnyaneshwari (commentary on the Bhagavad Gita) but face key challenges: • Time constraints: Busy professionals can’t commit to daily reading. • Complexity: Many find the language hard to read and understand. • Lack of accessibility: No simple, structured way to consume the text regularly. The goal: Make spiritual learning simple, accessible, and routine-friendly for modern devotees.

Solution

We launched Online Parayan with structured audio-video formats delivered via WhatsApp groups. The design allowed devotees to choose their own pace and created daily reminders for spiritual connection, without requiring new apps or complex setups.

About Product

Part of the Swayampoorna Foundation’s mission, Online Parayan was built to combine tradition with technology — making devotion accessible for youth, working professionals, and elderly followers alike, while fostering an engaged spiritual community.
