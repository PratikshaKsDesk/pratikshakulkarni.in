üìñ About EPIC

Our Vision

To create a world-class ecosystem that nurtures 10,000 exceptional Product Leaders who will shape the future of technology and innovation. We envision a community where aspiring product professionals have access to industry-standard knowledge, real-world experiences, and mentorship from successful product leaders.

Our vision extends beyond traditional education - we aim to create a sustainable platform where knowledge flows freely between industry veterans and emerging talent, fostering innovation and excellence in product management.

Our Mission

Our mission is to structurally create product mindset and equip professionals with essential skillsets through:

  • Industry-driven curriculum designed by active product leaders
  • Hands-on immersions in real product development scenarios
  • Continuous learning through webinars, workshops, and masterclasses
  • Direct mentorship and guidance from experienced practitioners
  • Building a supportive community of product professionals
  • Bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements
