E-Learning App – Connecting Learners with Experts Beyond Traditional Classrooms

Context

Many people want to learn specific skills and niche topics outside standard curriculums but struggle to find accessible, credible platforms or mentors. On the other side, subject-matter experts lack a centralized platform to share knowledge, host classes, and monetize their expertise effectively.

Solution

We scoped and designed an E-Learning platform to bridge this gap. The app provides structured video lessons, live classes, notes, and Q&A features, ensuring learners get personalized experiences while experts gain tools to monetize and scale their teaching.

About Product

This app aims to democratize learning beyond classrooms. It creates a curated, credible space where learners discover experts easily and experts host and monetize classes seamlessly.
